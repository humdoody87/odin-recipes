Original Homemade Italian Beef

Traditional Italian Beef

This is a Chicago style italian beef and is a Chicago classic and favorite.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 3 1 ounce packages of dry Italian dressing mix
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 16oz jar of pepperoncini peppers
  • 8 hamburger buns split

Steps

  1. Place the roast into a slow cooker, and season with Italian dressing mix.
  2. Pour in the water.
  3. Cover, and cook on High for 6 to 7 hours.
  4. During the last hour, shred the meat with two forks - if it does not shred easily, cook longer.
  5. Add the peppers, and as much of the juice as you like for additional flavor.
  6. Serve on buns.
